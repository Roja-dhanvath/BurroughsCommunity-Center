Reservations

Burroughs has a variety of rooms available for organization to use to host trainings, meetings or program activities. The meeting spaces are available for small parties, business meetings and seminars. Meeting rooms can accommodate up to 2 to 80 people depending on the set-up and configuration. The Center has rooms available for use for events and meetings of all sizes. We have facilities suited to a wide range of activities, from small group meetings to ballroom dance to batting practice. We can also provide catering for your event.
